Accessibility Policy



Introduction


Welcome to Apna Bank's Accessibility Policy. We are committed to providing a website that is accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. This policy outlines our efforts to ensure an inclusive online experience for everyone.


Compliance with Accessibility Standards


Apna Bank strives to meet or exceed the accessibility requirements outlined in applicable laws and standards, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. We are continuously working to improve accessibility on our website.


Accessible Features


We have implemented various features to enhance website accessibility, including:

Text Alternatives: Providing text alternatives for non-text content, such as images and multimedia.

Keyboard Navigation: Ensuring that all website functionality can be operated via keyboard.

Readable Text: Maintaining a readable and legible font size and ensuring sufficient contrast between text and background.

Descriptive Links: Using descriptive link text to provide context to screen readers.

Adaptive Design: Employing responsive design principles to ensure our website is usable on a variety of devices and screen sizes.


Testing and Evaluation


We regularly conduct accessibility audits and testing to identify and address accessibility issues. We welcome feedback from our users to help us improve accessibility further.


Third-Party Content


While we strive to make our website accessible, some third-party content or services may not be fully accessible. We encourage users to report any accessibility issues they encounter with such content.

Accessible Format Requests

Feedback


We welcome feedback from our users about the accessibility of our website. Your input helps us identify and resolve accessibility issues.


Commitment to Improvement


Apna Bank is committed to making continuous improvements to our website's accessibility. We value accessibility as a fundamental aspect of our online presence and are dedicated to ensuring that all users have equal access to the information and services we provide.
